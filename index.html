SEGERSTROM CENTER FOR THE ARTS

Costa Mesa is OC's home for performing arts! Each season, Costa Mesa welcomes award-winning and fan favorite Broadway shows in the city's South Coast Metro area, including those like "Hamilton," "Dear Evan Hansen," "Phantom of the Opera" and "Cats." With Segerstrom Center for the Arts' beautifully crafted theater and intimate feel, Costa Mesa is the ideal place to plan a weekend getaway to enjoy your favorite show.

SOUTH COAST REPERTORY

South Coast Repertory is a professional resident theater and one of the best kept secrets for an intimate setting for viewing new plays. SCR's extensive new play development program consists of commissions, residencies, readings, and workshops, from which up to five world premieres are produced each season.

COSTA MESA ART WALK

California Scenario, known by locals as "Noguchi Garden", brings various California geographies together in a sculptural oasis of indigenous plants and materials. It's one of the highlights on Costa Mesa's Art Walk, where you can take a free audio tour of 20 different incredible art pieces around the city, by internationally renowned artists.

VACA SOUTH COAST METRO

Headed by “Top Chef” front-runner Amar Santana, Vaca dishes up a Spanish style menu of tapas, paellas, mouthwatering steaks and ocean-caught seafood. The flavorsome menu is matched with one of the most impressive wine and cocktail lists in town and an unmatched ambiance that combines Spanish and California cool in perfect harmony.

OCMA

The biggest grand opening of the decade came to Costa Mesa in Oct. 2022, when the architecturally stunning Orange County Museum of Art opened in the Theatre & Arts District. Enjoy 4,500 pieces in the collection of modern art plus rotating exhibitions. Best of all, admission is free thanks to a generous endowment by Lugano Diamonds.
